# Car Rental Management System (CRMS)

DriveEasy Rentals owns and runs a network of car rental branches at three different
locations within the city (Downtown, Airport, and Westside). Currently, vehicle
availability, customer registrations, rental check-outs/check-ins, and payment
records are managed manually at each branch location using paper records and
spreadsheets. This process leads to problems such as double bookings, inaccurate
records, and delayed customer service. Due to the growing demand for vehicle
rentals, the company now needs to automate and centralize its rental management
operations.

The new system will have a web-based application to allow staff to check-out and
check-in vehicles. The system will also maintain information about each branch,
including name, address, and phone number, to enable the company to manage its
fleet across all locations. All vehicles in the system are classified under a car
type (such as Sedan, SUV, Van, Truck etc.), to enable customers to find vehicles
that match their needs and budget.

All vehicles in the system have a unique VIN number, in addition to the vehicle's
plate number. The vehicles are identified in the system by their VIN number. The
system allows registered customers to search through available vehicles to find
the desired car based on type and availability.

The system will retain information on all registered customers, including their
name, address, phone number, email, and driver's license number. Only registered
customers with a valid driver's license are allowed to make reservations and rent
vehicles. Customers can make or cancel reservations online and view their booking
history. The system will keep track of the outstanding balance owed by each
customer. No customer will be allowed to make a new reservation if they have an
outstanding balance on their account.

The system will either run on a server hosted by the company or hosted in a cloud
service. Staff and customers will be able to gain access into and use the system
through a web-browser interface. However, only staff members are able to process
vehicle check-outs, check-ins, and payments. Managers are able to manage
vehicles, customers, branches, and view basic reports on rental history and
vehicle availability across all branches.
