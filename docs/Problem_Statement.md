# 1. Project Description / Problem Statement
   ## 1.1 Background

The car rental industry requires efficient systems to manage vehicles, reservations, customers, and payments across multiple branches. Many mid-sized rental companies still rely on manual record keeping, paper forms, phone calls, and spreadsheets to manage daily operations. While these methods may work for smaller businesses, they become inefficient and error-prone as the company grows and customer demand increases.

DriveEasy Rentals is a growing car rental company that operates three branches located at Downtown, Airport, and Westside. Currently, vehicle availability, customer registrations, rental check-outs/check-ins, and payment records are managed manually at each branch location. Staff members often communicate through phone calls or paper records to verify vehicle availability and customer information. This process leads to problems such as double bookings, inaccurate records, delayed customer service, poor tracking of overdue returns, and loss of revenue.

To improve efficiency and business operations, DriveEasy Rentals requires a centralized and automated Car Rental Management System (CRMS). The new system will provide a web-based application that allows staff and customers to access the system through a web browser. The system will maintain records of vehicles, branches, customers, reservations, rentals, payments, and rental history in a centralized database shared across all locations.

The system will also maintain detailed information about each vehicle, including vehicle ID, registration number, brand, model, category, rental rate, fuel level, mileage, and availability status. Customers will be able to search available vehicles based on category and branch location before making reservations.

Only registered customers with valid driver’s licenses and identification documents will be allowed to rent vehicles. The system will support vehicle check-out and check-in operations, automatically calculate rental fees, overdue penalties, fuel charges, and damage costs, and track outstanding balances owed by customers. Customers with unpaid balances above a specified limit or overdue rentals will not be allowed to rent additional vehicles until their accounts are cleared.

The system may be hosted on a company server or cloud platform, allowing authorized staff and customers to securely access the system online from different branch locations.

## 1.2 Project Goals

The goal of this project is to design and implement a web-based Car Rental Management System (CRMS) that centralizes and automates rental operations for DriveEasy Rentals.

The system will:

- Manage vehicle inventory across all branches with real-time vehicle availability updates.
- Maintain detailed records of vehicles, vehicle categories, branch locations, and rental pricing.
- Store customer information including addresses, phone numbers, driver’s license details, and payment information.
- Allow registered customers to search available vehicles and create reservations online.
- Enable staff to perform vehicle check-out and check-in operations efficiently.
- Track vehicle mileage, fuel levels, damages, and maintenance information during rentals.
- Automatically calculate rental charges, overdue fees, fuel charges, and damage penalties.
- Prevent customers with overdue rentals or unpaid balances above the allowed limit from making new rentals.
- Maintain payment records and outstanding balances for all customers.
- Provide reports for managers including revenue reports, rental history, vehicle usage statistics, and branch performance.
- Provide a secure web-based system accessible by customers and staff through browsers.
- Improve operational efficiency, reduce manual errors, and enhance customer service across all company branches.
