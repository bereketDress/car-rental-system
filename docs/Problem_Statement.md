# 1. Project Description / Problem Statement

## 1.1 Background

DriveEasy Rentals operates three branches (Downtown, Airport, and Westside) and currently manages vehicle rentals manually using paper records and spreadsheets. This causes problems such as double bookings, inaccurate records, and delayed customer service.

The goal is to build a simple web-based Car Rental Management System (CRMS) that allows customers to register, search for available vehicles, and make or cancel reservations online. Staff can process vehicle checkouts and check-ins, and record payments. Managers can manage vehicles, customers, branches, and view basic reports. All data is stored in a central database shared across all branches.

## 1.2 Project Goals

The goal of this project is to design and implement a web-based Car Rental Management System (CRMS) that centralizes and automates rental operations for DriveEasy Rentals.

### The system will:

- Manage vehicle inventory across all branches with real-time vehicle availability updates.
- Maintain records of vehicles, vehicle categories, and branch locations.
- Store customer information including name, address, phone number, email, and driver's license number.
- Allow registered customers to search available vehicles and make or cancel reservations online.
- Enable staff to process vehicle check-out and check-in operations.
- Automatically calculate rental charges based on daily rate and rental duration.
- Record and track payments for each completed rental.
- Maintain outstanding balances for customers.
- Provide basic reports for managers, including rental history and vehicle availability across branches.
- Provide a secure web-based system accessible by customers and staff through a browser.
- Improve operational efficiency, reduce manual errors, and enhance customer service across all branches.
