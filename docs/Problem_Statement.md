# 1. Project Description / Problem Statement
## 1.1 Background

The car rental industry requires efficient systems to manage vehicles, bookings, and payments. Many mid-sized rental companies still use manual methods or disconnected tools like paper records and spreadsheets, which can lead to errors such as double bookings, poor tracking, and lost revenue.

This project, the Car Rental Management System (CRMS) for DriveEasy Rentals, is designed to solve these issues. The company operates across three branches (Downtown, Airport, and Westside) with over 200 vehicles. Currently, all rentals are managed manually, and staff must communicate by phone or paper records to check availability. This becomes inefficient as the business grows.

## 1.2 Project Goals

The goal is to build a web-based system that centralizes and automates rental operations. <br> 
The system will: <br>
*Manage vehicle inventory across all branches with real-time availability updates.  <br>
*Store customer details and ensure only registered users can rent vehicles. <br>
*Allow staff and customers to create and manage reservations easily. <br>
*Support vehicle check-out and check-in, including mileage, fuel level, and damage tracking. <br>
*Automatically calculate rental charges, late fees, fuel charges, and damage fees. <br>
*Handle payments and prevent rentals for customers with high outstanding balances. <br>
*Provide reports for managers, such as revenue, fleet usage, and rental history. <br>
